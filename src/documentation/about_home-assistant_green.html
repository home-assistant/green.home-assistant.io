Smart home hub for Home Assistant

Home Assistant Green is a smart home hub with a preinstalled Home Assistant operating system. Because smart home hubs usually run around the clock, Green is designed to have a low power consumption: under heavy load, it uses ~3 W, 1.7 W when idle. It also has a small footprint, allowing you to store it in any small space near a power outlet and an Ethernet connection.

Features

Affordable

Green is the easiest way to run Home Assistant for the low price of ~$100.

Easy installation

Just plug in power and network cables, and Green will take care of the rest. Our mobile apps let you setup seamlessly.

One system to control everything

Home Assistant combines your Apple, Google, Samsung, and Amazon devices with support for hundreds of thousands of other smart devices - all in one app.

Extensible

You can add Home Assistant SkyConnect for Zigbee/Thread, Home Assistant Cloud for voice assistants, and third-party accessories to support other smart standards.

Powerful smart home hub hardware

Green has a powerful 1.8 GHz quad-core ARM processor with 4 GB RAM and 32 GB eMMC storage - just what you need for Home Assistant.

Monthly updates / open source

Home Assistant is built by one of the largest communities of open-source developers. New features and improvements are added every month.

Privacy

Home Assistant keeps your data locally, which means both private from companies and accessible to you.
